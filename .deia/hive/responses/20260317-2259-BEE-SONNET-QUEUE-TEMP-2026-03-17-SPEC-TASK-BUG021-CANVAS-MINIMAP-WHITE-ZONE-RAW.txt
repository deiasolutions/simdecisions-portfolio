### What Was Fixed
The Canvas minimap's visible zone indicator was showing white on dark backgrounds, making it hard to see. The corner outline was also misaligned.

### Solution
Updated `canvas.css` to override ReactFlow's default white stroke with theme-aware CSS variables:

```css
.react-flow__minimap-mask {
  stroke: var(--sd-purple) !important;
  stroke-width: 2;
  stroke-dasharray: 4 4;
  fill: none !important;
}
```
