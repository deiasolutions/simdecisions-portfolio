### Architecture Verification

**Slot Reservation Flow:**
1. Queue runner polls `/build/slot-status` → {available: 7}
2. Queue runner reserves slots: `POST /build/slot-reserve {spec_id: "spec-001", bee_count: 3}`
3. Hivenode updates: `slot_reservations["spec-001"] = 3`, available = 10 - 3 = 7
4. Queue runner dispatches spec (already has reservation)
5. Bees complete → queue runner releases: `POST /build/slot-release {spec_id: "spec-001", released: 3}`
6. Hivenode updates: removes "spec-001" from reservations, available = 10

**Oversubscription Handling:**
- Reservations can exceed capacity (allows queuing ahead)
- `get_slot_status()` caps `available` at 0 (never negative) for safety
- Queue runner respects 0 available = no new submissions

**Timeout/Failure Handling:**
- Queue runner releases slots on timeout (line 622)
- Heartbeat auto-releases claims on terminal status (line 274 in build_monitor.py)
